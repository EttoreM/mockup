Neighbourhood Flood Vulnerability Index (NFVI) provides insight into the social vulnerability of a neighbourhood should a flood occur. The NFVI combines five characteristics of vulnerability:

  • Susceptibiity
  • Ability to prepare
  • Ability to recover
  • Ability to recover
  • community support

Map categories are given according to scores in the index with Acute indicating areas where social vulnerability is highestin a UK context: Acute, Veru High, relatively High, average (UK mean), relativelu Low, Very Low, Slight. See the technical guide for more information
